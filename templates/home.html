Understanding Risk

What Causes Landslides?

Our system monitors the three most critical factors. Here is why each one matters.

⛰️

Steep Slopes

Gravity constantly pulls on hillside material. The steeper the slope, the less force it takes to trigger a collapse. Slopes above 30 degrees are high risk.

Low angleVery steep
🌧️

Heavy Rainfall

Water seeps into cracks, adds weight, and creates pore pressure that pushes soil particles apart. Prolonged rain is the #1 trigger worldwide.

Light rainExtreme
💧

Soil Moisture

Saturated soil becomes heavy and loses cohesion — it literally turns into flowing mud. Moisture above 60% makes slopes dangerously unstable.

Dry soilSaturated
